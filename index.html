📍 About Us

M&M Holdings is a proudly Southern African real estate company headquartered in Plumtree, Zimbabwe, with operations extending into South Africa. Our mission is to help individuals and families own land, build homes, and invest in their future through reliable, affordable, and transparent services.

We specialize in selling stands, houses, plots, and farms, as well as project managing construction work across low-density and high-density suburbs.

Whether you’re a first-time buyer or a seasoned investor, we walk with you from the moment you deposit to the moment you build.

🛠️ Our Services

  • ✔️ Stand Sales – In both high-density and low-density areas
  • ✔️ House Sales – Affordable ready-built homes
